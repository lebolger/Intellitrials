Future Forward

So, what's next? This quarter's trends point to a future defined by the robot revolution in AI, a scramble to secure supply chains, and insights from the industry's biggest brain-dumps (aka conferences).

The AI Revolution is Here

AI is becoming the industry's secret sauce. From the FDA's "Elsa" tool speeding up reviews to Roche's new AI research hub, it's clear AI is reshaping everything. But with great power comes great responsibility, and the industry is also grappling with the "dual-use" threat and the need for ethical guardrails to prevent misuse.

Making Drugs Closer to Home

The pandemic was a wake-up call. Now, pharma giants like Novartis ($23B) and Roche ($50B) are investing heavily in US-based manufacturing. This isn't just about patriotism; it's a strategic move to de-risk fragile supply chains and ensure a stable supply of critical medicines. Expect more "Made in the USA" labels on your meds.
